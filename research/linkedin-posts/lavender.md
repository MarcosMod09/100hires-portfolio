### Insights

- Strong execution often comes from **leveraging external expertise**  
- Trust and proven results matter more than broad vendor options  
- Top performers build **curated networks**, not random partnerships  
- Different parts of the revenue engine require specialized skills  
- Relationships drive long-term performance, not just tools  

---

### Actionable Tactics

- Identify gaps in your outbound system:
  - lead generation  
  - messaging  
  - sales process  
- Build a small network of:
  - trusted partners  
  - proven operators  
- Prioritize:
  - quality of partnerships over quantity  
- Regularly evaluate:
  - who actually drives results  
- Avoid trying to solve every problem internally
