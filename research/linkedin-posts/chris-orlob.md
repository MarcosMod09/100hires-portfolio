### Content

After working with thousands of revenue teams, Chris identifies 5 patterns of top-performing sales orgs:

1. Focus on where they win (clear ICP)
2. Strong narrative that resonates with buyers
3. Performance-driven culture
4. Defined sales stages with clear exit criteria
5. Continuous skill development

Core idea:
Great sales teams combine **focus, clarity, and capability**.

---

### Insights

- Winning teams are highly selective with their ICP  
- Messaging (narrative) directly impacts conversion  
- Performance culture requires both high standards and transparency  
- Clear process improves forecasting and execution  
- Skill development is a continuous system, not a one-time effort  

---

### Actionable Tactics

- Define and track:
  - where deals are won/lost  
- Refine messaging until it clearly matches buyer problems  
- Set transparent performance expectations  
- Build structured sales stages with:
  - clear entry/exit criteria  
- Invest in ongoing training:
  - roleplay  
  - coaching  
  - skill tracking
