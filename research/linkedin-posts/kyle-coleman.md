### Content

Kyle shares an early story about optimizing a simple task (writing with two pencils) driven by a discomfort with wasted time.

That mindset carried into his career:
- training SDRs manually worked at first  
- but became a bottleneck as the team scaled  

The solution:
→ document processes and build systems (wiki, playbooks)

Result:
- faster onboarding  
- more autonomy  
- regained time  

Core idea:
Productivity is not about working faster — it’s about building systems that scale.

---

### Insights

- Individual effort does not scale — systems do  
- Founders/managers often become bottlenecks without realizing it  
- Documentation and processes create leverage  
- Productivity is a **system design problem**, not a speed problem  
- AI amplifies those who already think in systems  

---

### Actionable Tactics

- Identify repetitive tasks:
  → document them into playbooks  
- Build internal systems:
  - onboarding guides  
  - training docs  
- Replace:
  - 1:1 repetition  
  with:
  - scalable processes  
- Regularly ask:
  → “Am I the bottleneck?”  
- Use AI to:
  - enhance systems  
  - not replace thinking
