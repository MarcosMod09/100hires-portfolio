# Outreach Templates

This section provides practical outbound templates based on patterns observed across top experts.

Each template is designed to be:
- short
- relevant
- low-pressure
- tied to real signals

---

## 1. Short Cold Email (Berman Style)

### Structure
- 2–4 lines
- one clear problem
- one simple CTA

### Template

Subject: quick question

Noticed you're hiring SDRs — usually means pipeline pressure.

We help teams increase qualified meetings without adding headcount.

Worth exploring?

---

## 2. Signal-Based Outreach (Morgan Style)

### When to use
- hiring activity
- company growth
- new roles

### Template

Saw you're hiring multiple SDRs — usually a sign pipeline is a focus right now.

We’ve helped teams increase inbound + outbound conversion without scaling team size.

Open to seeing how that works?

---

## 3. Low-Pressure Outreach (Braun Style)

### Principles
- conversational
- no pressure
- curiosity-driven

### Template

Quick question —

When teams try to improve outbound, is it usually more a messaging issue or a targeting issue on your side?

Curious how you're thinking about it.

---

## 4. Strong Offer-Based Message (Nick Abraham Style)

### Focus
- lead with outcome
- clear value upfront

### Template

We can generate a list of your ideal customers and build your first outreach campaign — free.

If it’s useful, we can talk next steps after.

Worth trying?

---

## 5. Follow-Up Message (Morgan + Farrokh)

### Rule
- change angle each time
- don’t repeat message

### Template 1 (Insight)

Following up — noticed your team is scaling quickly.

Usually that creates pressure on pipeline consistency.

Happy to share what we’ve seen work if helpful.

---

### Template 2 (Format Change)

Sent you a quick video breaking this down:

[link]

Thought it might be easier than a long message.

---

### Template 3 (Light Touch)

Not sure if this is relevant right now —

should I close the loop here?

---

## 6. Value-First / “Gift” Message (Morgan)

### Focus
- give before asking
- no immediate CTA

### Template

Put together a quick breakdown of your site + product positioning.

No pitch — just thought it might be useful:

[link / screenshot]

---

## 7. Personalization Using AI + Signals

### Example

Saw your job post for a Growth Manager — looks like you're investing in acquisition.

We’ve worked with similar teams to improve conversion from traffic to qualified leads.

Happy to share what that looks like if helpful.

---

## Key Principles Across Templates

- Short > long  
- Problem > product  
- Offer > description  
- Curiosity > pressure  
- Consistency > perfection  

---

## Final Insight

These templates are not meant to be copied exactly.

They are frameworks.

> The goal is to connect a real signal to a clear message — consistently.
