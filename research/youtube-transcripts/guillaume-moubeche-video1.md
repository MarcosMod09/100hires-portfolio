# Guillaume Moubeche — How to Get Customers with $0 Budget

Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WUw7BtR3I2U

---

## Summary

This video breaks down how to acquire customers without spending money by using outbound, communities, and content-driven prospecting.  
Guillaume Moubeche focuses on identifying the right audience, leveraging existing conversations, and building trust before selling.

---

## Key Outreach Strategies

- Focus on **who to target, when to reach out, and how to convert**
- Start with **people who already showed interest (hot leads)**
- Use **communities to build trust before selling**
- Leverage **existing engagement (comments, followers) as lead sources**
- Combine **automation + personalization at scale**

---

## Core Frameworks

### 1. Hot Leads (Highest Conversion)

People who:
- already discussed the problem with you  
- already showed interest  

Approach:
- follow up with a clear solution  
- reference previous conversation  
- keep it simple  

Example:
“Thanks for the earlier chat — I built something to solve [problem]. Worth trying?”

---

### 2. Community-Led Outreach

Find prospects inside:
- Facebook groups  
- Reddit  
- Slack communities  
- LinkedIn groups  

Strategy:
- engage first  
- offer value (even free work initially)  
- build trust → then convert  

---

### 3. Engagement-Based Prospecting

Target people who:
- commented on posts  
- liked content  
- follow specific creators  

Example:
“I saw you commented on [topic] — looks like this is relevant to you…”

This ensures:
→ higher relevance  
→ higher reply rates  

---

### 4. Cold Outreach with Context

Instead of random outreach:
- use a **trigger (post, comment, behavior)**  
- connect message to that trigger  

This makes outreach feel:
- timely  
- relevant  
- not random  

---

### 5. Social Proof + Iteration

Start by:
- offering value (even free work)  
- getting results  
- turning results into proof  

Then:
→ use proof to convert paying clients  

---

## Mistakes to Avoid

- Targeting everyone instead of a niche  
- Sending generic cold messages  
- Selling before building trust  
- Ignoring existing demand signals  
- Overcomplicating outreach  

---

## Actionable Steps

1. Identify your ICP (specific niche)  
2. Find where they are active (communities, LinkedIn, etc.)  
3. Start conversations or offer value  
4. Collect proof (results, testimonials)  
5. Use that proof in outbound messages  
6. Scale outreach using tools and automation  

---
