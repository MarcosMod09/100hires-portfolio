# Morgan J Ingram — LinkedIn Networking System (Power 20 Strategy)

Link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nRkvOjrUBTk

---

## Summary

This video explains a structured LinkedIn networking strategy focused on building high-value relationships instead of random outreach.  
Morgan J Ingram introduces the “Power 20” system, where you focus on a small group of high-impact people to generate most opportunities.

---

## Key Outreach Strategies

- Focus on a small group of high-value targets (Power 20)  
- Engage consistently instead of random networking  
- Use comments to start conversations, not just react  
- Build relationships before making any ask  
- Measure success by conversations, not likes  

---

## Core Framework

### 1. Power 20 List (Targeting)

Instead of connecting randomly:
- identify 20 high-value people:
  - potential clients  
  - industry leaders  

Goal:
→ focus effort where it actually drives opportunities  

---

### 2. Strategic Engagement

Use the **2-sentence rule**:
- 1 insight  
- 1 question  

Example:
“Interesting take on cold calling — I like the opener you mentioned.  
Curious, what’s your conversion rate with that approach?”

Goal:
→ start conversations, not just leave comments  

---

### 3. Relationship Building (90-Day Rule)

Breakdown:

**Days 1–30**
- consistent engagement (comments, likes)

**Days 30–60**
- start conversations (reply, DM naturally)

**Days 60–90**
- move toward:
  - collaboration  
  - meetings  

Goal:
→ build trust before asking  

---

### 4. Measure What Matters

Track:
- conversations started  
- DMs received  
- collaborations  

Not:
- likes  
- comments volume  

---

## Mistakes to Avoid

- Random networking and connection requests  
- Leaving low-value comments (“Great post”)  
- Asking for meetings too early  
- Tracking vanity metrics instead of conversations  
- Trying to scale before building relationships  

---

## Actionable Steps

1. Create your “Power 20” list (ideal customers + creators)  
2. Spend 30 minutes daily engaging with their content  
3. Use insight + question in every comment  
4. Build relationships over time (don’t rush)  
5. Track conversations and inbound messages  

---

## Key Insight

The biggest shift is:
**from volume-based outreach → relationship-based targeting**

Instead of:
- reaching out to hundreds of random people  

You:
- build trust with a small, high-impact group
